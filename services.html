FAQ

Common Questions

How much does it cost? +
Websites start at $3,500 and increase based on your requirements, page count, and functionality. Ad management starts at $1,000/month (management fee only; your ad spend budget is paid separately directly to Google or Meta). After our free strategy call, I'll put together a clear, itemized proposal with no hidden fees.
Do I need both services? +
No. You can start with just a website or just ad management, depending on what your business needs most right now. That said, they work best together: a great website makes your ads more effective, and ads drive traffic to your website. I'll recommend what makes the most sense for you.
How long does a website take to build? +
Typically 2–4 weeks from our initial discovery call to launch, depending on complexity and how quickly we can gather your content (photos, copy, etc.). I keep you updated throughout and won't launch until you're satisfied.
How long until I see results from ads? +
Ads can start driving traffic within days of launching. However, the first 2–4 weeks are an optimization period. I gather data, test what works, and refine the campaigns. Most clients see meaningful results within the first month.
What do I need to provide? +
For a website: your logo (if you have one), photos, and any existing content. I can help with copy and suggest stock imagery if needed. For ads: access to your ad accounts (I'll help you set these up if you don't have them) and any promotions or messaging you want to highlight.
Is there a contract? +
Ad management is a month-to-month agreement with no long-term lock-in. Website projects are one-time engagements with clear deliverables. You're never trapped. If you're not happy with the results, we talk about it and figure it out together.
