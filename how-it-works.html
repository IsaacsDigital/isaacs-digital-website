FAQ

Process Questions Answered

Do I need to sign a long-term contract? +
No long-term contracts. Ad management is month-to-month, and website projects are one-time engagements with clear deliverables. I earn your continued business by delivering results, not by locking you in.
How involved do I need to be? +
Minimal. I need your input at the start (for the strategy call and content gathering) and your approval before anything launches. After that, I handle everything and keep you informed through regular reports. You run your business — I handle the digital side.
What if I'm not happy with the results? +
If results aren't meeting expectations, we have an honest conversation about it. I'll review what's happening, explain my analysis, and adjust the strategy. I'm invested in your success — unhappy clients aren't something I'm okay with. For specific concerns about refunds, see our Refund Policy.
Can I pause or cancel ad management? +
Yes. Since it's month-to-month, you can pause or cancel with reasonable notice. I'll make sure you have full access to your own ad accounts at all times so you're never held hostage to my services.
Who owns the website after it's built? +
You do, 100%. Your website, your domain, your hosting account. Everything built for you belongs to you. If you ever want to move on, you take everything with you, no strings attached.
