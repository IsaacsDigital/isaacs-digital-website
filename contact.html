Before You Reach Out

Quick Answers

Is the strategy call really free? +
Yes, 100% free. The 15-minute strategy call costs you nothing and commits you to nothing. It's genuinely just a conversation to understand your business and figure out if and how I can help.
How fast will you respond? +
I typically respond within a few hours during business hours. If you reach out outside of business hours, I'll get back to you first thing the next business day. I take response time seriously.
I don't have a big budget. Should I still reach out? +
Yes. I work with businesses of different sizes and budgets. I'll be honest with you about what's realistic for your budget and whether it makes sense to move forward. The strategy call is the best way to find out.
I already have a website. Can you help with just ads? +
Absolutely. You can hire me for ad management only, website only, or both. If you have a website I'll take a look at it during our call and give you honest feedback on whether it's set up to convert the traffic the ads will drive.
Do you work with businesses in different areas? +
Yes. I work with local businesses across the country. Digital services don't require us to be in the same city — everything can be done remotely, and I'm experienced helping local businesses in a wide range of markets.
